About Me

Welcome! I'm Harry Zhu, a first-generation Computer Science student at the Georgia Institute of Technology. With a strong foundation in software development and a deep passion for innovation, I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities for growth!

I am particularly interested in Information Internetworks and Systems & Architecture due to the challenges of building scalable systems while ensuring robust network communication.

Beside my academic pursuits, I enjoy staying updated with the latest trends in technology, participating in hackathons, and building projects. When I'm not immersed in code, you can find me playing basketball, cooking, or reading. My reading interests vary widely, from the latest advancements in technology to my favorite fantasy novel, Harry Potter.
